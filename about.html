About

Grimpixels.com is a place for my side projects. Comics, illustrations, web projects, etc. For now, it's where I'll be publishing my comic Food, Fame & Fury.


Food, Fame & Fury is a satirical dark comedy about a lonely food delivery man who spirals into obsession with a webcam model. Struggling to find help for his declining mental state, he turns to the internet, leading him down an even darker path.

Characters

Tarvus

Tarvus (not travis) is an unstable, e-bike riding, junk-food-pushing socially awkward loner hungry for human connection.


Breezy

Breezy is a bubbly webcam model craving fame and validation, looking for love in all the wrong places.


Vik

Vik is a charismatic, manipulative self-absorbed tech billionaire with political aspirations always cooking up schemes to fill the void within.
